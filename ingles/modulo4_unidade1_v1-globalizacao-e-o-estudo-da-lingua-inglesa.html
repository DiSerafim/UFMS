Improving Communication Skills

Ask for repetition

- i'm sorry, could repeat that please?
- Could you repeat the last part of your sentence?
- What did you say before/after? 

        * me desculpe, poderia repetir por favor?
        * Você pode repetir a ultima parte da sua frase?
        * O que você disse antes/depois?

Not sure about it?

Ask for clarification

- Sorry, i don't understand the word you used
- Could you clarify that, please?
- Could you explain what you mean by...? 

        * Desculpe, não entendi a palavra que você usou
        * Você pode esclarecer, por favor?
        * Você poderia explicar o que você quer dizer com...?

Check Understanding by paraphrasing

- Are you saying that...?
- Did you mean...?
- I thing i understand you... Am i right? 

        * Você está dizendo isso...?
        * Você quis dizer...?
        * Acho que entendo... estou certo?

Dos and Don'ts

Don't exceed in:
- talking around.
- talking too fast

DON'T use the same question over and over.
Try to vary as much as you can. 

        * Não use a mesma pergunta repetidamente.
        * Tente variar.

Try to use a synonym, gestures, mines or a drawing.
