Colloquialism

A linguagem coloquial, informal ou popular:
- a bunch of numpties - a group of idiots;
- go bananas - go insane or be very angry;
- y'all - you all;
- look blue - look sad;
- buzz of - go away;
- wanna - want to;
- gonna - going to;

Fillers, Correction and Repetition

Expressões para preencher o discurso, pausas e repetições:
- "uh";
- "ummm";
- "well"

Word or phrase clusters

Agrupamento de palavras "chuncks" conjunções e preposições:
- Chunk #1: dad eating dinner.
- Chunk #2: the phone rang.
- Chunk #3: furious.
- Chunk #4: a telemarketer.
- Chunk #5: always call.
- Chunk #6: sitting down to dinner.
