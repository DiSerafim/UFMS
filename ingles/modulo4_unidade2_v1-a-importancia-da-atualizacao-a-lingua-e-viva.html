Learning Objectives

In this class, you will...

- Become familiar with the most common questions asked in a job interview.
- Be introduced to ways for answering work related questions.
- Receive tips on how to express yourself professionally.

Challenge

Answer questions related to your personal and professional experiences during a job interview.

How to succeed in a job interview

- Check the school website.
- Rehearse in front of the mirror.
- Get ready to answer anything (unexpected).
- Feel confident to talk about strengths and weaknesses.
- Emphasize your most significant work or school experiences, your future plans and your ideal job.
- Dress properly.
